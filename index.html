Pyramidion Cryptocurrency LLC

A closed barter network wallet built on crypto rails.

PYRA is a distributed-ledger barter credit system for approved members, designed for trade activity, account approval, activity records, and multichain transparency.

Website and member app coming soon

Protocol Basics

PYRA is not presented as a conversion service. It operates as a barter credit and unit of account inside a closed barter network.
